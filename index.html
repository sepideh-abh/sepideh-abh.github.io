About me

Hi! I am Sepideh, a 22-year-old Physics student from Tehran, Iran. I am an undergrad at the Sharif University of Technology, and It's in my last year now.
The most significant part of physics for me is that you can find it anywhere, biology, chemistry, and all engineering fields. It's almost impossible to take even a small step toward solving a problem in any of mentioned fields without using physics.
I find that nothing is more rewarding for me than approaching a problem in a way no one has done before, like designing an experiment and being an engineer in a physics lab. To design an experiment, you have to use all aspects of physics. That's why I found experimental physics to be so appealing. My favorite aspect of experimental physics is that some questions cannot be answered by theoretical physics alone (at least not in our lifetimes!). In such cases, the answer might be found in the laboratories.
